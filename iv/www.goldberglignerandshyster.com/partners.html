TOM GOLDBERG Esq

Mr. Goldberg should be credited as much for his human rights and charity work as he is for his considerable successes within the legal industry.

Often functioning as a legal expert on Weazel News and quoted in the Daily Globe on many occasions, Mr. Goldberg has become the voice of the 'little guy' in Liberty City and throughout America.

When not working on cases with GL&S, the majority of Mr. Goldberg's time is spent working with Goldberg's Guys - his charity dedicated to teaching the disadvantaged young boys of Liberty City to swim. "When I see the look in those kids eyes when they're kicking their little legs around, it's all worthwhile." Tom Goldberg: Humanitarian, Moral Compass, Attorney with Goldberg Ligner and Shyster.
