Peep That Shit



Cluckin' Bell has the best TV commercials ever. Have you seen them? And what great food. They are always careful to keep the secret ingredient of their tasty batter - salt - under wraps. It is amazing how they managed to take one of the world's healthiest meats and turn it into a lethal death trap. Forget avian flu - Cluckin Bell's deep-fried carrion has been killing people for decades. A visit to Cluckin' Bell leaves a thin film of grease and dirt all over your body that takes days to wash off... and that's why we love it. Stop off for a Mighty Cluck. Only 99c! That's cheaper than water! Or try the Stuffed Pollo Todo Frito. It's an entire chicken stuffed with mashed potatoes and gravy then deep fried! Awesome.
